University Admission


  • Step 1 Information and advice

Making sure you know what courses are available and which might suit you best is obviously a key part of what we do. Regardless of what stage you’re at – whether you know nothing about the UK, Canadian, Australian or New Zealand universities, or have already narrowed things down to a shortlist – we’re here to help.


  • Step 2 Provide application forms

Once you’ve decided where you want to apply, we give you the application forms and an easy-to-follow guide on how to apply. If your chosen course has any extra requirements – such as a portfolio, evidence of work experience, or a statement of purpose – we’ll let you know. We will also point out any specific application deadlines.


  • Step 3 Making an application

Once you are ready to go, pass your application to one of our overseas offices which in close to you or email it to us. We check them to make sure everything is complete and correct before sending them off to the universities for assessment. We’ll make certified copies of your original academic documents to include with your application, and return the originals to you using recorded delivery. Like everything else we do, this is a free service.


  • Step 4 Application processing

After making certified copies of your documents, we submit your applications by email or upload them directly to the admissions systems online, so applying via us is actually quicker than any other means. As we’re the official representative for UK, Canada, Australia & New Zealand universities, applications sent by our office are fast-tracked through the admissions process, so you’ll get an answer more quickly than if you applied directly.


  • Step 5 Provide a safety net

Applying via us reduces the risk of being rejected because of a mistake on your application form, or queries on your academic background. If the admissions teams require more information or want to question something, they will contact us for clarification, so you’ll get a chance to explain whatever the issue is. If you have an issue further down the process – a last-minute change of heart about your course choice, or when you want to start, for example – we will liaise with the University for you to sort it out.

Help with each and every step of the process

From deciding where to apply and submitting applications to accepting a place, help with applying for a student visa, sorting out accommodation at any point of the process, just call in one of our offices or email one of the team.
